# Architectural Decision Record Template

> Every design choice has a reason. Document it, or you'll forget why — and make the same mistake again.

```markdown
# ADR-[NNN]: [Decision Title]

**Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
**Status**: [proposed | accepted | deprecated | superseded]
**Deciders**: [who was involved]

## Context

[What situation or problem prompted this decision?]

## Options Considered

### Option A: [Name]
- **Pro**: ...
- **Con**: ...

### Option B: [Name]
- **Pro**: ...
- **Con**: ...

## Decision

[Which option was chosen and why.]

## Consequences

- [What changes as a result]
- [What new constraints this creates]
- [What becomes easier / harder]

## Revisit Conditions

[Under what circumstances should this decision be reconsidered?]
```

## Tips

- **Write ADRs at the moment of decision**, not after. Context fades fast.
- **"Superseded by ADR-NNN"** is better than deleting old decisions. The trail matters.
- **Include what you rejected** — knowing what you didn't choose teaches as much as what you did.
