## Exercise 3: Define Your Gates (10 min)

Gates are absolute rules — conditions that override the score.

| Gate | Condition | Result | Real example |
|------|-----------|--------|-------------|

## Discussion Prompts (Study Group)

1. **"Compare your axes."** How do group members' values differ? What does that say about their systems?

2. **"Score the same decision with different frameworks."** Pick one scenario and have everyone score it. Where do the frameworks agree? Where do they diverge?

3. **"What's your most controversial gate?"** A rule that would surprise other builders.
